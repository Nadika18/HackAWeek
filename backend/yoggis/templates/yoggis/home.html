What is exactly Yoggis?

Yoggis is a website which aims at improving the mental and physical health of people in Pandemic times.It is a yoga trainer in your own devices which helps you to perform yoga poses and estimates the accuracy.

How does it work?

The machine learning model calculates the accuracy of the yoga postures based on some calculations at the back-end.

Can anyone use it?

Yes! Anyone sitting at their homes can access the website with just a click.
